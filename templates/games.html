Number Memory Game
This game will demonstrate where you are in terms of number memorization. In short, this game will starts off with a number that will dissapear from your screen. You will then have to write down the number in the box and click submit. If you are correct, you move on to the next number, and gain a score point. If you do not, you are met with a game over and that'll be it for that session. Additionally, after 3 successful guesses, the number will add on a new number to increase the challenge of memorization.
